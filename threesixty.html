360KID Flash Game



360KID made a 5th and 6th grade math textbook into an interactive game/lesson for internet and DVD. Promoted from Production Artist to Team Leader, directly under Production Manager. These are templates, each element has a seperate, editable symbol to make new charts and graphs easy to create, and to create consistently.

I used my large-team textbook publishing experience to make sure we were all creating Flash chapters with a consistent look and feel, to avoid long and costly re-work. I provided a standard color palette and fonts in the art director’s style, and suggested palette tweaks to the production manager who was making the XML structure.
